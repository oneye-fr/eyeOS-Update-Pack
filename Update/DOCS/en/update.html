update
------

To update eyeOS do this:

 1. Download the latest version of eyeos from www.eyeos.org.
 2. Uncomress the package.
 3. Erase the directories "etc", "home" and "usr" from the folder in numer 2.
 4. Upload all the other files to your Webspace.
    Replace all old files with its new.
